WELCOME TO TRAVEL WITHOUT TRAFFIC

About

Travel without traffic is an application for user to book bus ticket such that to reserving seats

For traffic police - it is used to monitor traffic at signals and change the timings

Admin - uses this application to add new bus, manage booking, list all booking, delete a bus

How to book ticket:

User can sign up / login and go to book section in which had to type from and to names of the city

Then it lists the available buses and enter the bus id to book your ticket....

How to simulate traffic light:

As of now police can insert the picture captured from camera for n -side traffic signal click predict after each picture insertion

Then finally click on simulate and then again click on start simulation to start.then press stop to stop simulation and change time
